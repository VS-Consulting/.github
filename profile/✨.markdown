**🔮 *The Odoo.sh Ritual* 🔮**

# *Monday Morning.*

```
Steam rises from
a cup of Italian certainty.
Bitter.
Precise.
Just like the week ahead.

We open the gates —
a branch named
`release-w25`.

Not twenty-five thousand euros to invoice,
no—
just a humble number,
marking the week
when we awaken,
log in,
and summon the next incarnation
of the code.

Next Monday:
`release-w26`.
Like clockwork.
Like ritual.
Like Gregorian monks,
we chant commits
into the abyss of time.

But beware the solstice week.
No ordinary name shall hold.
We carve it in ancient runes:
`release-⚔️`.

Because that week,
code flows through legend,
and the year turns.
New dawn,
new branch:
`release-w1-2027`.

And so,
the wheel turns again.

But only
after coffee.
Always
after coffee.
```
